🌱 Plant Care Guide

Learn how to take care of your plants the right way

💧

Watering

Water your plants based on their type.

Overwatering can damage roots.

☀️

Sunlight

Place plants where they get proper light.

Some need direct sunlight, others indirect.

🌡️

Temperature

Keep plants in moderate temperature.

Avoid extreme heat or cold.

🌿

Soil

Use nutrient-rich and well-drained soil.

Different plants need different soil types.

🪴

Repotting

Repot when plant outgrows its pot.

Usually every 6–12 months.

🍃

Cleaning

Wipe leaves to remove dust.

Helps plants breathe better.

Quick Tips 🌱

• Check soil moisture before watering

• Rotate plants for even growth

• Avoid placing plants near AC vents

• Use organic fertilizers occasionally
